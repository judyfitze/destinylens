## Paid Ad Launch Plan

### Phase 1: Test ($10/day, 7 days)

**Platform:** Facebook/Instagram  
**Objective:** Traffic to sales page  
**Budget:** $10/day  
**Audience:**
- Age: 25-45
- Interests: Entrepreneurship, online business, personal development
- Behaviors: Engaged shoppers
- Location: US, Canada, UK, Australia

**Creative Test:**
- 3 short-form video hooks
- 2 long-form ads
- 5 headline variations

**Success Criteria:**
- CTR > 2%
- CPC < $1.50
- Landing page view cost < $2

---

### Phase 2: Validate ($25/day, 14 days)

**If Phase 1 hits criteria:**
- Increase budget to $25/day
- Kill underperforming creatives
- Scale winners
- Add retargeting pixel

**New Audiences:**
- Lookalike of landing page visitors
- Interest stacking (entrepreneurship + self-improvement)
- Broad targeting with CBO

**Success Criteria:**
- Cost per purchase < $25
- ROAS > 2.0

---

### Phase 3: Scale ($50-100/day)

**If Phase 2 hits criteria:**
- Increase budget 20% every 3 days
- Add YouTube ads
- Test Google Ads (search: "dream life calculator")
- Expand to new countries

**Daily KPIs:**
- Spend
- CTR
- CPC
- Landing page views
- Add to carts
- Purchases
- ROAS

## Scale Triggers

**Scale UP (double budget):**
- 3 consecutive days with ROAS > 2.0
- CTR consistently > 3%
- Cost per purchase < $20

**Scale DOWN (pause and fix):**
- ROAS < 1.5 for 2 days
- CTR < 1% for 3 days
- Cost per purchase > $40

**Kill Creative:**
- CTR < 1% after 1,000 impressions
- No purchases after $50 spend
- Negative comments overwhelming
