# DestinyLens — Business Blueprint (Stage 2)

## Product Overview

**Name:** DestinyLens  
**Tagline:** "Your Dream Life In Focus"  
**Price:** $47 USD (one-time)  
**Model:** One-time purchase, lifetime access  

---

## Core Offer

DestinyLens is a visual command center that transforms abstract dreams into concrete daily income targets.

**The Problem:** Entrepreneurs have vision boards and vague goals ("financial freedom") but no concrete numbers driving daily action.

**The Solution:** A 9-question calculator that reveals exact Dream Life Numbers (monthly/weekly/daily/hourly) + a visual dashboard to track progress in real-time.

---

## Unique Positioning

| Competitor | DestinyLens Difference |
|------------|------------------------|
| Budgeting apps (YNAB, Mint) | Not about restricting — about expanding |
| Goal trackers (Notion templates) | Purpose-built with income integration |
| Spreadsheet calculators | Beautiful, emotional, visual command center |
| Vision board apps | Tied to actual income tracking |

**Key Differentiator:** The circular progress gauge that fills with purple/gold as income flows in — gamified, emotional, visual.
