Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: In DestinyLens
  1. Go to Income Connections
  2. Click "Connect Stripe"
  3. Enter a label (e.g., "My Business")
  4. Click Connect
  5. Copy the webhook URL above ☝️
Step 2: In Stripe Dashboard
  1. Go to dashboard.stripe.com
  2. Click Developers → Webhooks
  3. Click "Add destination" (top right)
  4. Select event type: Checkout → Checkout session completed
  5. Click Next
  6. Select "Webhook Endpoint"
  7. Click Continue
  8. Configure Destination:
    • Name: (e.g., "DestinyLens")
    • Paste your webhook URL
  9. Click Create destination
✨
Done!
Your Stripe payments will now automatically appear in DestinyLens

Setup Instructions:

In Stripe Dashboard:
  1. Go to dashboard.stripe.com/webhooks
  2. Click "+ Add endpoint"
  3. Paste your webhook URL above
  4. Check "Select all events" (or pick any events - we handle them all)
  5. Click Add endpoint
In PayPal Dashboard:
  1. Go to developer.paypal.com/dashboard
  2. Select your app under "Apps & Credentials"
  3. Scroll to "Webhooks" section
  4. Click "Add Webhook"
  5. Paste your webhook URL above
  6. Select event: Payment capture completed
  7. Save and copy the Webhook ID

💡 After adding the webhook, your connection status will update automatically when the first payment arrives.
